The news comes as wellness businesses across Bellingham report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said Ashley White, clinic founder in Bellingham.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 14 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 7 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — Ashley White

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
