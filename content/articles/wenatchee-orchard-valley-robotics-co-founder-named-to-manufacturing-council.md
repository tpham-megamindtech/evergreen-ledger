The announcement is the latest sign of momentum for Wenatchee's business community, which has drawn growing attention from investors across the Pacific Northwest.

"It's a big step forward for Wenatchee, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said David Delgado, managing partner.

The company now employs roughly 62 people across its Washington operations, with plans to keep hiring if growth holds through next year. Leadership says Wenatchee's talent pipeline was a deciding factor in choosing to expand locally rather than out of state.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — David Delgado

Company leadership says an update on hiring and expansion plans is expected before the end of the year.
