The news comes as wellness businesses across Renton report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Renton, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Laura Ramirez, clinic founder.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 24 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 5 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Laura Ramirez

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
