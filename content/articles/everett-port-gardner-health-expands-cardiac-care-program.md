Health officials in Everett say the move is meant to keep pace with a fast-growing population and rising demand for local care.

"It's a big step forward for Everett, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Laura Ramirez, nursing director.

The expansion is expected to add roughly 34 new positions, including clinical and support staff, and comes as regional population growth pushes demand for care to record levels.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Laura Ramirez

Patients are encouraged to check with their provider directly for scheduling as the new services come online.
