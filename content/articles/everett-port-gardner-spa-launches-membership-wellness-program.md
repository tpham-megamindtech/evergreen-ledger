The news comes as wellness businesses across Everett report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Everett, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Amanda Garcia, master stylist.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 18 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 11 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Amanda Garcia

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
