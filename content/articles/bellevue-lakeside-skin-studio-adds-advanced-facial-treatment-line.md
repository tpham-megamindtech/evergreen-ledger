The news comes as wellness businesses across Bellevue report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said James Anderson, licensed esthetician in Bellevue.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 11 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 4 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — James Anderson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
