The announcement is the latest sign of momentum for Redmond's business community, which has drawn growing attention from investors across the Pacific Northwest.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Tyler Moore, chief operating officer in Redmond.

The company now employs roughly 54 people across its Washington operations, with plans to keep hiring if growth holds through next year. Leadership says Redmond's talent pipeline was a deciding factor in choosing to expand locally rather than out of state.

> "We're proud of what this means for Redmond and the people who live here." — Tyler Moore

Company leadership says an update on hiring and expansion plans is expected before the end of the year.
