The announcement is the latest sign of momentum for Renton's business community, which has drawn growing attention from investors across the Pacific Northwest.

"It's a big step forward for Renton, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Ashley White, chief operating officer.

The company now employs roughly 59 people across its Washington operations, with plans to keep hiring if growth holds through next year. Leadership says Renton's talent pipeline was a deciding factor in choosing to expand locally rather than out of state.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Ashley White

Company leadership says an update on hiring and expansion plans is expected before the end of the year.
