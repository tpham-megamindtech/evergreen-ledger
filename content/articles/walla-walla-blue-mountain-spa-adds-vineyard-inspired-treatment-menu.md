The news comes as wellness businesses across Walla Walla report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said Emily Clark, spa owner in Walla Walla.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 20 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 13 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — Emily Clark

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
