The news comes as wellness businesses across Vancouver report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Chris Robinson, clinic founder in Vancouver.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 19 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 12 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Vancouver and the people who live here." — Chris Robinson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
