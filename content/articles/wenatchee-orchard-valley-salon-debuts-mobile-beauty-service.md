The news comes as wellness businesses across Wenatchee report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Daniel Nguyen, spa owner in Wenatchee.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 25 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 6 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Wenatchee and the people who live here." — Daniel Nguyen

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
