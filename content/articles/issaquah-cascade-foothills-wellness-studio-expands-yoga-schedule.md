The news comes as wellness businesses across Issaquah report steady demand from longtime regulars and a growing number of newcomers drawn to the Puget Sound lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Megan Walker, wellness director in Issaquah.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 22 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 3 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Issaquah and the people who live here." — Megan Walker

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
